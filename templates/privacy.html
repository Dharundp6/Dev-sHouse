User Rights

Users have certain rights regarding their personal data, which include:

  • Right to Access: Users have the right to request access to their personal data stored by us and receive information about how it is processed.
  • Right to Rectification: Users can request the correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Users have the right to request the deletion of their personal data under certain circumstances, such as when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Users can request the restriction of processing of their personal data in certain situations, such as when they contest the accuracy of the data or when processing is unlawful.

To exercise these rights or make inquiries about their personal data, users can contact us using the contact information provided in the "Contact Information" section below.

Cookies Tracking

We use various types of cookies and tracking technologies to enhance user experience and analyze website traffic. These may include:

  • Session Cookies: These are essential for the operation of our website and expire once the user closes their browser.
  • Preference Cookies: These cookies store user preferences and settings to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use analytics cookies to track and analyze how users interact with our website, which helps us improve its performance and content.
  • Marketing Cookies: These cookies are used to display personalized advertisements based on user behavior and interests.

Users can manage their cookie preferences or opt-out of tracking by adjusting their browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the website. For more information on how we use cookies, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

We take the security of user data seriously and employ various measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • Access Control: We restrict access to user data to authorized personnel only and employ authentication mechanisms to verify user identity.
  • Firewalls and Intrusion Detection Systems: We deploy firewalls and intrusion detection systems to monitor network traffic and prevent unauthorized access to our systems.
  • Data Backup and Redundancy: We regularly back up user data and maintain redundant systems to minimize the risk of data loss in the event of hardware failures or disasters.

By implementing these measures, we strive to maintain the integrity, confidentiality, and availability of user data and provide a secure environment for our users.
